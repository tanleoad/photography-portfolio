The Photographer

About

Abu Dhabi, UAE

Tanleo_

Tania Khan is a photographer and visual storyteller, born & raised in Abu Dhabi, specializing in street and architectural imagery with a strong focus on cinematic color grading. Her work explores the relationship between light, mood, and color, transforming everyday scenes into intentional visual narratives.

Since beginning her photography journey in 2019, she has developed a distinctive style rooted in storytelling through composition and post-processing. Her work has been exhibited internationally at Fulle Galleria in Argentina and was also recognized in the Spaces of Light competition at Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.

Her photography has been featured on the official Instagram platforms of Qasr Al Hosn, the Cultural Foundation, and the Film Abu Dhabi Commission, and she has contributed to commercial and corporate projects — including photographing the Ethara corporate suite for the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix. Her work has also been published in What's On Abu Dhabi, further establishing her presence within the regional creative and cultural landscape.

Through her workshops and creative practice, Tania shares her approach to color grading as a tool for shaping emotion and elevating imagery beyond documentation into intentional visual storytelling.
