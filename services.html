What I Offer

Services

Work With Me

Photography & retouching, shaped by colour

Every project — whether a street series, a portrait sitting, or an architectural study — is approached the same way: composition first, light second, and a cinematic grade to tie it together.

01

Street Photography

Candid, observational images that capture the energy, light and rhythm of everyday life across Abu Dhabi. Ideal for personal collections, editorial features, or brand storytelling that calls for an authentic, in-the-moment feel.

02

Portrait Photography

One-on-one sessions built around natural light and intentional composition, resulting in portraits that feel cinematic rather than staged. Available for individuals, couples, and small creative teams.

03

Architectural Photography

Considered studies of interiors, facades and architectural detail, shot to highlight symmetry, scale, and the interplay of light across a space. Suited to hospitality venues, cultural institutions, and commercial properties.

04

Face Retouching

Careful, natural-looking retouching that refines skin, tone and light while preserving texture and character. Available as part of a shoot, or as a standalone service for your existing images.

05

Color Grading Workshops

Small-group and 1:1 sessions sharing Tania's approach to color grading as a tool for shaping emotion — elevating imagery beyond documentation into intentional visual storytelling.
